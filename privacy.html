Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Cory Wong, operating as a broker with Trustwell Realty Inc., Brokerage ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects personal information through the website portal.corywong.ca (the "Site").

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information through this Site:

  • Analytics data: When you consent to cookies, we use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect anonymized usage data including pages visited, time on site, referring URL, browser type, device type, and approximate geographic location. This data is processed by Google LLC.
  • Cookie consent preference: We store your cookie consent choice locally in your browser so we do not ask repeatedly.

2. How We Use Information

Analytics data is used solely to understand how visitors interact with the Site so we can improve its content and usability. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies

This Site uses cookies only when you provide explicit consent:

  • Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _ga_*): Used to distinguish users and throttle request rate. These cookies are set by Google and persist for up to 2 years.

You may withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser's cookies and local storage for this site.

4. Google Analytics and Data Processing

Google Analytics collects data that is transmitted to and processed by Google in the United States. Google may use this data in accordance with its own Privacy Policy. We have configured GA4 to anonymize IP addresses where possible.

5. Your Rights Under PIPEDA

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for the collection of your information
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

6. Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted. Cookie consent preferences are stored locally in your browser with no expiration.

7. Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
